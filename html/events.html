Safe blood saves lives
Every month, The GIVING charity organises blood donation events in different locations in Jordan. We work together with local partners, Red Cross branches and volunteers to bring these events close to the donors. The Blood Service donation has permanent offices in four cities: Amman, Irbid , Azzarqa and Mafruq. Where are the blood donation events organised? We visit nearly 60 different locations and organise a total of about 100 blood donation events each year. The choice of the location is affected by the amount of eligible donors living in and around the location, and other such factors. Charity Bazaar
Crafts, handicrafts, practices and various products, made by hands who work in their homes, participate in a charity bazaar. The proceeds go to support the poor families and orphans. By marketing these products, it aims to support poor families and orphans, especially the poor students in the less fortunate regions.
